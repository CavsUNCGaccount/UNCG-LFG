User Posts

Welcome to the User Posts page!

This page is for gamers to make a post to find others to play with. Jump into the action quicker without creating and organizing a full group.
Or just hang out with other gamers to discuss anything related to the game!

To post, simply type your message in the text area below and click the "Post" button.
Your post will be visible to other users, and they can respond to you if they are interested in joining your gaming session.
You can also view other users' posts and respond to them if you are interested in joining their gaming session.
Please be respectful and considerate when interacting with other users.

Click here to see suggestions on what to include in your post

  • The platform you are playing on (PlayStation, Xbox, or Steam).
  • Your gametag for the platform you are on.
  • How many other gamers you need for a gaming session.
  • If you would like other gamers to have a mic or not.
  • The time that you want to play. (To find players who can play at the same time).
  • Skill level or experience (Casual, Competitive, Pro, New Players welcome).
  • Preferred game mode (Co-op, Ranked, Casual, PvP, PvE, etc.).
  • When you are usually online (specific days or times).
  • Any other preferences (e.g., "Chill players only", "No toxic behavior", "Beginner-friendly").
