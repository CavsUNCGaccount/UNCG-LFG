About UNCG LFG

UNCG LFG is a platform for students to find gaming groups, schedule gaming sessions, and meet like-minded players.

Features

  • Find players for your favorite games
  • Schedule gaming sessions
  • Join gaming sessions hosted by other players
  • Game Community pages for 20 popular co-op/multiplayer games
  • Achievement tracking for Steam users
