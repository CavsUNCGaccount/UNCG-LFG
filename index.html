About UNCG LFG

UNCG LFG is a platform for students to find gaming groups, schedule gaming sessions, and meet like-minded players. UNCG LFG focuses on PlayStation, Xbox, and Steam platforms.

Features

  • Find players for your favorite games
  • Schedule gaming sessions
  • Join gaming sessions hosted by other players
  • Game Community pages for 20 popular co-op/multiplayer games of the last 15 years. All of these games are available for PlayStation, Xbox, and Steam.
  • Display your PlayStation, Xbox, and Steam gamertags.
  • Achievement tracking for Steam users.
