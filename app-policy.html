App Policy

Our guidelines to ensure a safe and friendly community

Behavior Guidelines

To maintain a positive and welcoming environment, all users are expected to follow these guidelines:

  • Respect all members, regardless of their background or opinions.
  • Avoid using offensive language, hate speech, or discriminatory remarks.
  • No harassment or bullying of any kind.
  • No cheating or exploiting game mechanics.
  • Do not share or promote inappropriate content.

Consequences of Violating Policies

Breaking the rules will result in the following consequences, depending on the severity:

  • Warning - A notification to stop the toxic behavior.
  • Temporary Ban - A temporary suspension from the platform.
  • Permanent Ban - Complete removal from the platform without the possibility of return.

Reporting and Appeals

If you encounter any toxic behavior, you can report the user through the app. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team.

If you believe a ban was made in error, you can submit an appeal. Our team will review your case and respond accordingly.
